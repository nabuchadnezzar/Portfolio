Riada Finance offers a wide range of services in the field of financing of businesses’ current operations in form of secured and unsecured loans, providing for strict confidentiality of all information about the financial transactions.

Unsecured loans can be made available for up to 180-day term in amount not exceeding three million rubles.

To have a bigger amount provided for a longer period it is necessary to submit a collateral in form of a third party’s surety or pledge of real estate, vehicle, equipment or special-purpose machinery.

Guarantee by legal entities or physical persons is also needed.

Parameters of each particular financing deal are defined upon review of constitutive documents and study of accounting and managerial reports of potential borrower.
