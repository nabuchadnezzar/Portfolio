Riada Finance offers a wide range of services for direct financing of the current activities of companies in the form of unsecured and secured loans, while ensuring the confidentiality of all information about financial transactions.

Unsecured loans can be provided for up to 180 days in an amount not exceeding three million rubles.

To receive a larger amount for a longer period, it is necessary to provide security in the form of a guarantee of a third party or a pledge of real estate, vehicles, equipment, special equipment. A surety of legal entities or individuals is also required.

The parameters of each specific financing transaction are determined based on the results of consideration of the constituent documents and analysis of the accounting and management reports of the potential borrower.
